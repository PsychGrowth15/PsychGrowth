OUR TEAM

At PsychGrowth, we are a passionate and dedicated team committed to creating inclusive, supportive, and empowering spaces for neurodivergent individuals and their families.

  • Special Educators with years of classroom and home-based intervention experience
  • Psychologists and Counsellors trained in child, adolescent, adults and family mental health
  • Successful Neurodivergent Individuals who bring lived experience and powerful insights into neurodiversity
  • Experienced Teachers and Parents who understand the daily challenges and victories in raising or teaching neurodiverse children

Together, we combine empathy, expertise, and evidence-based practices to support every individual’s unique growth journey.

Founder – Malka Tarannum

Counsellor and Therapist | Founder of PsychGrowth

I am a counselling psychologist with a Master’s degree in Counselling Psychology and a proud mother of a neurodivergent child. My personal and professional experiences have shaped my deep understanding of the needs of neurodiverse individuals and their families.

With training in CBT, DBT, ABA, child and adolescent therapy, and over 4 years of experience working as a teacher, trainer, mentor, counsellor, and therapist I have developed a holistic, Eclectic therapeutic approach. I specialize in:

  • Providing counselling and therapy to neurodiverse individuals (Autism, ADHD, etc.)
  • Training and empowering parents
  • Supporting individuals with anxiety, depression, emotional and behavioural challenges
  • Creating customized home-based therapy plans and educational tools

PsychGrowth is the realization of my mission to provide compassionate, customized, and empowering mental health support. Here, healing begins with understanding — and growth happens with support.
